About me

I'm interested in all aspects of user experience, especially interaction design and quantitative user research. My time living and working overseas has also stimulated a deep interest in user experience across different cultures.

In my past careers, I've been a technical writer and a linguist. Technical writing equipped me with both a deep appreciation of good interface design and skills in communicating information to users. Studying linguistics allowed me to hone my talents in observing users interacting with complex systems. My career in UX gives me the chance to expand these skills to new domains.
